So much music so little time

Loafers are a Northwest band specializing in classic rock 'n roll dance charts punctuated by the sizzle of live horns. We're driven by a killer rhythm section and guitar, stylish vocals, and feature the musical power of horns, harmonicas and keyboards that can please the most discriminating tastes. Depending upon the venue size and type, we may highlight an expanded horn section or feature guest vocal talent. With over a hundred combined years of professional music experience and over a hundred tunes in our repertoire we can customize the world of rock 'n soul to fit any need like an old pair of shoes Loafers.

Rocking the Northwest for many years, our members represent one of the most musically diverse backgrounds in rock 'n roll music you will ever find. From major symphony orchestras and small classical chamber groups, to jazz ensembles and dixieland bands our members have traveled throughout the world sharing the joy of music with cultures from around the globe. Combining advanced degrees in performance and music education with over a hundred years of performance experience making for a rock band just like the one you've never heard.
